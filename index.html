Meals

Breakfast

3 whole eggs, 1 cup egg whites, ¾–1 cup dry oats, one banana or berries, and 8 oz whole milk.

12:30 shake

1 scoop Body Fortress Isolate. Use 8–12 oz whole milk on lifting days; water on recovery days.

Dinner

10–12 oz cooked 93/7 or leaner ground beef, 1½–2 cups cooked rice, vegetables, and Greek yogurt when needed.

Target: roughly 185–200 g protein. Lifting days: about 2,600–2,750 calories. Recovery days: about 2,400–2,500.
Supplements

Jekyll & Hyde: On lifting mornings, start with 1 blue scoop from the pink side + 1 red scoop from the white side in water, 15–30 minutes before training. That is half of each label serving. Do not combine with coffee or other stimulants.

Body Fortress Isolate: One scoop at midday. A second scoop only when food will not get you near the protein target.

Clean Gainer: Not automatic. Use half a labeled serving only when you are clearly short on calories, especially after legs or chest/back. Mix with water and reduce dinner rice if needed.

Do not exceed any label serving. Stop stimulant use for chest pain, faintness, severe dizziness, or sustained palpitations.
Training rules

Compounds: stop with 1–2 clean reps left. Isolation exercises: the last set may reach technical failure.

Rest 2–3 minutes for heavy lifts and 60–90 seconds for accessories. Add weight only after reaching the top of the rep range with clean form.

Incline finisher means walking: 10–15% incline, about 2.8–3.3 mph.
